Overview

For our second year projects class, myself and five other class mates were to create an invoice tracking system for Warman Homes Center. Below are some screen shots of the program with a description. Due to the size of the project, there are only a few images describing some of the fucntionality

This is the main login screen where you can login as an administrator or a shipper.

Loggining in as an administrator allows you to edit current employees or create new ones, as well as editing new trucks or creating new trucks.

You can edit a persons name as well as thier "type" or position in the company

Once you hit save, the employee is updated and saved to the Database

This Is the main login screen where the shipper can schedule, create, and manage invoices as well as contacts and their information.
